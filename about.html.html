Our Story 🌟

Karupi Pet Store was born out of pure love for animals. Founded in Puducherry, we started with a small shop and a big dream — to make pet adoption joyful, safe, and accessible for every family in India.

Over the years, we've grown into one of South India's most trusted pet destinations, offering over 100+ breeds of dogs, cats, birds, fish, reptiles and more — all health-certified, vaccinated, and ready to become your best friend.

Every pet in our store is treated like family. We believe that when you adopt from us, you don't just get a pet — you gain a lifelong companion and a support system that cares deeply about your pet's wellbeing.
