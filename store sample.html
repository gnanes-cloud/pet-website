Pet Health Tips 🌿

Expert-approved advice from our in-house vets to help your furry, feathery, and scaly friends live their best, healthiest lives.

🐕
Dog Health Tips
Keep your canine companion healthy & happy
💉
Vaccination Schedule
Essential
Keeping your dog's vaccinations up to date is the single most important thing you can do for their health.
  • Core vaccines: Distemper, Parvo, Hepatitis at 6–8 weeks
  • Rabies vaccine at 12–16 weeks, then annually
  • Bordetella (kennel cough) if your dog meets other dogs
  • Keep a vaccination card and show it to your vet annually
🍗
Dog Nutrition Guide
Diet
A balanced diet is the foundation of your dog's long-term health, energy, and coat quality.
  • Feed age-appropriate food (puppy, adult, senior formula)
  • Avoid grapes, raisins, onions, garlic, chocolate — all toxic!
  • Provide fresh water at all times
  • Measure portions to prevent obesity — a leading health issue
  • Treats should not exceed 10% of daily caloric intake
🏃
Exercise & Mental Health
Wellness
Physical and mental stimulation prevent destructive behavior and keep your dog joyful.
  • Small breeds: 30 min walk daily; large breeds: 60–90 min
  • Use puzzle feeders to stimulate the mind
  • Socialization with other dogs and humans is crucial
  • Swimming is excellent low-impact exercise for older dogs
🪮
Grooming Basics
Grooming
Regular grooming prevents skin issues and helps you spot early signs of health problems.
  • Brush coat 2–3× per week; daily for long-haired breeds
  • Bath every 4–6 weeks with dog-safe shampoo only
  • Trim nails monthly to prevent joint pain
  • Clean ears weekly; check for redness or odour
  • Brush teeth 3× per week to prevent dental disease
🐱
Cat Health Tips
Feline care essentials from our expert vets
💊
Deworming & Parasite Control
Essential
Cats are prone to internal and external parasites. Regular prevention is key.
  • Deworm kittens every 2 weeks until 3 months old
  • Adult cats: deworm every 3–6 months
  • Use vet-recommended flea/tick treatments monthly
  • Check coat regularly for flea dirt or unusual scratching
🥩
Cat Diet & Hydration
Diet
Cats are obligate carnivores. Their diet must be rich in animal protein to thrive.
  • Never feed dog food — cats need taurine found only in cat food
  • Wet food helps with hydration; cats rarely drink enough water
  • Avoid milk — most adult cats are lactose intolerant
  • Toxic to cats: onions, garlic, xylitol, raw dough, caffeine
🧼
Litter Box Hygiene
Hygiene
A clean litter box is crucial for your cat's health and preventing stress-related illness.
  • Scoop the litter box once or twice daily
  • Replace litter fully and wash the box weekly
  • One litter box per cat, plus one extra (n+1 rule)
  • Place boxes in quiet, accessible locations
  • Changes in litter habits can signal urinary issues — see a vet
🦜
Bird Health Tips
Expert care for your feathered friends
🌱
Bird Nutrition
Diet
A seed-only diet is a common mistake. Birds need variety for optimal health.
  • Pellets should form 60–70% of diet; seeds only as treats
  • Safe fruits: apple, mango, papaya (remove seeds)
  • Safe veggies: carrots, leafy greens, sweet potato
  • Never give: avocado, chocolate, caffeine, alcohol, onion
  • Fresh water daily; clean water dish thoroughly
🏠
Cage & Environment
Environment
Birds are sensitive to their environment. The right setup prevents stress and disease.
  • Cage bars should be horizontal for climbing; wide spacing for safety
  • Minimum 3–4 hours of supervised out-of-cage time daily
  • Keep away from kitchen fumes — Teflon/PTFE fumes are deadly to birds
  • Maintain room temperature 20–26°C; avoid drafts
  • 10–12 hours of sleep in a quiet, covered cage
🐠
Fish & Aquarium Tips
Keep your aquatic world crystal clear and healthy
🧪
Water Quality Management
Critical
Water quality is everything in fishkeeping. Most fish deaths are caused by poor water conditions.
  • Cycle your tank for 4–6 weeks before adding fish
  • Change 25–30% of water weekly
  • Test water weekly: pH, ammonia, nitrite, nitrate
  • Use a good filter rated for 2× your tank volume
  • Dechlorinate tap water before adding to tank
🍽️
Feeding Your Fish
Diet
Overfeeding is the #1 mistake fishkeepers make and quickly degrades water quality.
  • Feed only what fish can consume in 2–3 minutes
  • Feed 1–2 times daily; skip one day per week
  • Vary diet: flakes, pellets, frozen bloodworms, veggies
  • Remove uneaten food after 5 minutes with a net
💊
General Pet Care
Universal tips every pet parent should know
🩺
Regular Vet Checkups
Essential
Prevention is always better and cheaper than cure. Regular checkups can catch issues early.
  • Annual checkup for healthy adult pets
  • Twice yearly for seniors (7+ years) and young animals
  • Keep a pet health record or app to track visits
  • Don't wait for visible symptoms — subtle changes matter
🧠
Mental Wellbeing
Wellness
Mental health is just as important as physical health for all pets. Boredom leads to illness.
  • Provide enrichment toys and rotation of activities
  • Spend dedicated quality time with your pet daily
  • Watch for signs of anxiety: hiding, aggression, over-grooming
  • Consider a companion animal for social species
🏷️
ID & Microchipping
Safety
Hundreds of pets go missing every year. Simple precautions can save your pet's life.
  • Microchip your pet — a lifelong, permanent ID
  • Always use a collar with an ID tag showing your phone number
  • Update microchip registry if you move or change numbers
  • Take current photos of your pet every 6 months

🚨 Emergency Warning Signs — Act Immediately!

If your pet shows any of these signs, call your vet or rush to an emergency clinic right away:

😮‍💨 Difficulty breathing
🤢 Persistent vomiting or diarrhoea
💔 Pale or blue gums
😵 Collapse or loss of consciousness
🩸 Uncontrolled bleeding
🦴 Suspected fracture or injury
🌡️ Seizures or tremors
💊 Known or suspected poisoning
