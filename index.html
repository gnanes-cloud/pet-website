💡 Quick Pet Tips
💧
Fresh Water Daily
Always provide fresh, clean water for your pets every single day without fail.
🦷
Dental Hygiene
Brush your pet's teeth 2–3 times a week to prevent dental disease.
🏃
Daily Exercise
Dogs need at least 30 min of physical activity daily for a healthy life.
🩺
Annual Vet Visits
Schedule yearly check-ups even if your pet seems perfectly healthy.
🌡️
Watch for Symptoms
Loss of appetite, lethargy or unusual behaviour needs immediate vet attention.
